Story and Mission

Benchmarq focuses on meeting the needs of governments and organizations using big data and artificial intelligence to drive solutions that meet the needs of people across the globe. Our proprietary software platform offers an innovative approach for governments and other organizations. to help mitigate risk and be more proactive in providing goods and services to its stakeholders

We provide decision-makers with real-time data streaming, visualization and analysis tools in order to take a proactive approach to help mitigate future risk, increase key performance indicators, and improve the health and well-being of their citizens and other stakeholders
