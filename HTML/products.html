RISK

By using big data culled from a wide array of sources, we have developed our RISK (Real-time Information Streaming Kit) proprietary software platform that allows users to access thousands of exabytes of real-time data to crawl through a multitude of events, trends, analytics and news articles across the globe.

The Benchmarq RISK software kit integrates a wide variety of batch and streaming data that is updated automatically and easily accessible from your secure desktop and mobile devices.

The RISK software can continuously scan different regions all over the world to find out what events are currently happening; what people are discussing; how the economy is doing; determine the overall sentiment and tone of coverage; and how that tone may impact that region either positively or negatively.

INSIGHT

Sometimes, simply streaming real-time data is not sufficient to get a true sense of what’s happening on the ground in different areas throughout the world. Decision-makers need an array of tools at their disposal in order to make sense of the billions of data points and how they all fit together.

Our INSIGHT software solution takes real-time data and transforms these points of interest into customizable charts and graphs for detailed visualization. In addition, INSIGHT provides *smart text technology in the form of our proprietary AI algorithms that help you interpret and decipher your charts, graphs and other visuals, resulting in an easy to read document to be used in your final reports.

SAMME

Benchmarq strongly believes that governments and organizations work best when they use a proactive approach to risk mitigation. Instead of waiting for major crises to occur, these entities would do well to proactively prepare for a wide array of scenarios and expect some of these risks to occur with some degree of frequency. The question remains, however, which future scenarios should decision-makers expect and prepare for? Benchmarq’s flagship product is our Serverless Automated Machine-learning and Modeling Engine (SAMM-E). We developed state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms, powered by artificial intelligence, to help make predictions about political, social, economic, environmental, geospatial and other events based on historical batch and real-time streaming data.

Benchmarq SAMME is designed to harness real time, streaming data to give accurate forecasting and prediction models. Our team carefully calibrates unique machine learning models to deliver accurate results. We perform thousands of simultaneous calculations and “what-if” scenarios to improve robustness in forecasts.
